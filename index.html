About Zion Gates Academy

Founded in 2014, Zion Gates Academy is a premier primary school located in Transformer Kapchumba, Eldoret, Kenya, committed to providing quality education from Playgroup to Grade 9. Our mission is to nurture each child's intellectual, spiritual, physical, and social development.

We follow the Kenyan Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC) while integrating Christian values and 21st-century skills to prepare our students for future success. Our dedicated staff create a supportive and stimulating environment where children love to learn and grow.

At Zion Gates Academy, we believe every child is uniquely gifted and we strive to help them discover and develop their God-given talents.
